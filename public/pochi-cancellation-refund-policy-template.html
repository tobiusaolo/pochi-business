1. Definitions

  • Tourify: Tourify Technologies Ltd, operator of the Pochi e-commerce platform.
  • Pochi Platform: The digital marketplace connecting Customers and independent Sellers for the buying and selling of goods and services.
  • Customer: Any person or entity purchasing goods or services via Pochi.
  • Seller: Any independent business or individual offering goods or services on Pochi.
  • Goods: Products or services sold through the platform.
  • Platform Fee: Tourify’s service fee for facilitating transactions.

2. Platform Role

Pochi is a marketplace platform only and does not own, manufacture, store, or directly supply listed Goods.

  • Sales contracts are strictly between Customer and Seller.
  • Sellers independently determine cancellation, refund, return, and delivery policies, subject to Tourify approval and applicable laws in Uganda, Kenya, Tanzania, and Rwanda.
  • Tourify facilitates transactions and dispute resolution where applicable.

3. Policy Disclosure and Acceptance

Before purchase confirmation, Customers must receive:

  • Seller cancellation and refund summary
  • Full policy terms
  • Refund timelines and conditions
  • Required acceptance confirmation

By completing a purchase, Customers agree to both Seller terms and this platform policy.

4. Standardized Refund Policy Categories

All Sellers must adopt one approved policy category or submit a compliant custom policy.

General Conditions

Unless otherwise required by East African consumer protection laws:

  • Goods must be unused, unopened, and in original condition unless defective, damaged, or incorrectly delivered.
  • Proof of purchase is required.
  • Refunds are processed via original payment method.
  • Delivery or handling fees may be excluded unless Seller is at fault.
  • Exemptions may apply for perishables, customized goods, digital products, hygiene-sensitive goods, and final-sale items if clearly disclosed.

5. Seller Responsibilities

  • Clearly display refund terms before purchase
  • Honour selected refund obligations
  • Bear all financial responsibility for refunds, returns, and disputes
  • Comply with consumer laws of Uganda, Kenya, Tanzania, and Rwanda

Tourify may suspend or remove Sellers for policy abuse, non-compliance, or misleading practices.

6. Payment Processing

Where Tourify Processes Payment

  • Refunds follow Seller policy
  • Returned to original payment method
  • Standard processing: 7–14 business days

Where Seller Processes Payment Directly

  • Seller is solely responsible for refunds
  • Tourify may mediate disputes but bears no refund liability

7. Seller-Initiated Order Cancellations

If a Seller cancels a confirmed order:

  • Customer receives full refund
  • Platform Fees refunded where applicable
  • Seller may face penalties, suspension, or removal

8. Customer-Initiated Cancellations

Customers must cancel through:

  • Pochi account portal, or
  • Official customer support

Refunds are governed by Seller policy active at purchase time.

9. Disputes and Chargebacks

Tourify may facilitate:

  1. Initial review
  2. Seller response period
  3. Final platform determination for Tourify-handled payments

Where Seller fault is confirmed, Tourify may recover refund amounts from Seller.

10. Force Majeure

For events beyond reasonable control (government restrictions, unrest, disasters, public emergencies):

  • Sellers are encouraged to offer replacement, rescheduling, or store credit
  • Refunds remain subject to Seller policy and local law
  • Tourify may offer discretionary platform credits

11. Fees, Taxes, and Legal Compliance

  • Platform Fees may be refundable before Seller payout where applicable
  • Taxes refunded where legally required
  • Policies must comply with:
    • Uganda Consumer Protection laws
    • Kenya Consumer Protection Act
    • Tanzania Fair Competition and Consumer laws
    • Rwanda consumer commerce regulations

12. Policy Amendments

Tourify may update this policy at any time. Changes apply prospectively and will not affect completed purchases unless required by law.
